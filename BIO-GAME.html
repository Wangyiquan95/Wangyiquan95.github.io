BIO-GAME

Welcome to my collection of biology-themed games. These interactive games are designed to make learning biology concepts fun and engaging.

RNA Snake

A twist on the classic Snake game where you collect RNA nucleotides to build RNA sequences.

Evolution Simulator

Watch and influence how organisms evolve over generations in different environments.

Protein Folding Challenge

Learn about protein structures by solving protein folding puzzles.

Virus Containment

Control the spread of a virus by implementing containment strategies and learning about epidemiology.

Suika Journal

Inspired by Suika Game. Here you contribute journal papers and win Nobel Prize.

These games are designed to be both educational and entertaining. Feel free to explore them and learn more about the fascinating world of biology!
